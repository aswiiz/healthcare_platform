Empowering you with essential information on chronic conditions, wellness habits, and emergency response guidelines.

Cardiovascular Health

Preventative Habits

  • ✔ 150 mins of moderate cardio weekly.
  • ✔ High fiber, low-sodium dietary intake.
  • ✘ Avoid trans fats and processed meats.
  • ✘ Zero-tolerance for tobacco use.
Emergency: Cardiac Event
  1. Immediately dispatch emergency services.
  2. Position the patient comfortably sitting up.
  3. Loosen obstructive clothing around neck/chest.
  4. Keep patient calm until medics arrive.

Metabolic Wellness

Glycemic Management

  • ✔ Consistent glucose monitoring.
  • ✔ Emphasis on complex carbohydrates.
  • ✘ Avoid refined sugars and high-fructose syrups.
  • ✘ Never skip prescribed insulin or clinical meals.
Emergency: Hypoglycemic Shock
  1. Administer 15g of fast-acting carbs (juice/honey).
  2. Wait 15 mins and re-verify glucose levels.
  3. Verify consciousness; never feed an unconscious patient.
  4. Seek emergency help if levels remain unstable.

Holistic Vitality

Foundational Health

  • ✔ 8+ cups of verified hydration daily.
  • ✔ Minimum 7.5 hours of circadian rest.
  • ✘ Late-night blue light and caffeine intake.
  • ✘ Prolonged sedentary post-meal behavior.
Essential First Aid
  • Thermal Burns: Cool running water for 15+ minutes.
  • Soft Tissue: Use R.I.C.E method (Rest, Ice, Compress, Elevate).
  • Minor Scrapes: Flush with saline, apply sterile dressing.
